Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit today's entry?

Yes. Tap today's entry to open it and make changes at any time.

Does QuietDay require an account?

No. QuietDay works entirely without an account. Your entries are stored locally on your device.

Does QuietDay use AI?

No. QuietDay does not use AI in any form. Your entries are never processed or analyzed automatically.

Does QuietDay support iCloud?

Yes, if iCloud is enabled on your device and you have granted the app permission. Your entries will sync across your Apple devices through your personal iCloud account.

Can I delete my entries?

Yes. You can delete individual entries within the app. To remove all data, delete the QuietDay app from your device.
